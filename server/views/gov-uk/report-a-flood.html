Report a flood or possible cause of flooding (England)

Report flooding if you're in Scotland, Wales or Northern Ireland.

Who you need to contact depends on whats flooding.

Sewers and burst water mains

Contact your local water company to fix:

  • blocked sewers
  • burst water mains

Public drains and flooded roads

Contact your local council to:

  • clear public drains
  • report a flooded road

Rivers and the sea

Use this service to report:

  • a blockage, for example a fallen tree
  • collapsed or badly damaged river or canal banks
  • unusual changes in river flow
  • flooding from the sea or a main river - you can check which are main rivers.

Start now

Before you start:

  • you will need a location such as a national grid reference (NGR), property address or if on site a mobile phone with location services enabled.

Other ways to report a river or sea flood

Contact your local council to report flooding from other rivers, brooks or streams.

Groundwater flooding

Report groundwater flooding to your local council. Groundwater flooding is caused by water rising up through the ground.

Private drains

Floods caused by private drains are the responsibility of the property owner.
